Self-introduction


I received the B.S. degree in physics from Hunan Normal University, and the M.S. degree in computer science from the Hunan University of Technology. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with Central South University, working with Prof. Yaoxue Zhang. My research interests include Internet of Things, Edge computing, Data privacy and ecurity.
