Self-introduction


I received my Ph.D. degree with Central South University advised by Prof. Yaoxue Zhang in 2018. I received the B.S. degree in physics from Hunan Normal University and the M.S. degree in computer science from the Hunan University of Technology advised by Prof. Xiaoheng Deng. I have already published over 20 research articles in both journals and conferences, such as IEEE Transactions on Transactions on Information Forensics and Security, IEEE Network, IEEE Communication Magazine, IEEE IoT Journal, etc.



Research Interest

My research interests include Internet of Things, Edge computing, Privacy and Security for Big Data/Machine Learning.
